Maggie

Steps of Preparation:

  • 1 cup (approx 200–250 ml) paani pan me daalo
  • Gas on karo aur paani ko ubalne do
  • Maggi cake ko tod kar ya whole hi paani me daal do
  • Saath me tastemaker (masala packet) daal do
  • 2–3 minute tak medium flame pe pakao
  • Beech-beech me spoon se stir karo taaki chipke nahi
  • noodles soft ho jaye aur thoda sa gravy consistency aa jaye, gas band karo
